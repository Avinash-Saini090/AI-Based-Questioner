About Us

We are a team of final year students at SRMCEM, Lucknow, driven by a passion for facilitating self-directed learning. This project, titled "AI Based Questioner", is the culmination of our efforts to provide a valuable platform for students seeking to enhance their knowledge and practice their skills. Our Inspiration: The challenges faced by self-learners often include a lack of readily available practice material directly related to their learning resources. We understand the importance of reinforcing concepts through practice, and this project aims to bridge that gap. Our Mission: Our mission is to empower self-learners by providing a user-friendly platform overflowing with practice questions. Whether you're studying from video lectures, uploaded documents, or any other learning source, our platform strives to offer a plethora of relevant questions to solidify your understanding. Our Team: Avinash Saini, Azahruddin Mohammad Hassan, Our Guide: Dr. Ashish Awasthi, SRMCEM, Lucknow, has provided invaluable guidance and support throughout the development of this project. His mentorship has been instrumental in shaping our vision and ensuring its successful execution. Looking Ahead: We are committed to continuously improving this platform and expanding its capabilities.
